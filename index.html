CASL Compliance Notice

CASL s.6(5) — Implied Consent (B2B)

This system contacts only business addresses sourced from public professional registries. Under CASL section 6(5), implied consent applies when:
• The recipient's business contact information is publicly available
• The message is relevant to their business or professional role
• The sender clearly identifies themselves and provides an unsubscribe mechanism

Required in every outreach: Your full name, business name, phone number, and a sentence offering to remove them from your list.

What this system does NOT collect: Personal emails, residential phone numbers, home addresses. All data sourced from CPSO public registry and hospital public directories.
